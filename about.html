Meet John Cornforth

With years of competitive experience and a deep understanding of the game, John Cornforth brings a professional, results-driven approach to squash coaching in Tauranga.

John's coaching philosophy centers on holistic player development, combining technical excellence with tactical awareness, physical conditioning, and mental resilience. Every session is tailored to the individual, ensuring measurable progress and genuine enjoyment of the sport.

Based at the premier Devoy Squash & Fitness facility, John works with players of all ages and abilities, from juniors taking their first steps in the sport to competitive adults seeking to elevate their game.
